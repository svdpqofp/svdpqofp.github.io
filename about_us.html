Serving the community of Mesa, Arizona since 1954.
Members of the Society of St. Vincent de Paul (Vincentians) are men and women striving to grow spiritually by offering personal service to those in their local community in need. Queen of Peace Vincentians represent all ages, every race, and all incomes, and all have a unique awareness that it is their duty to share their many blessings with their neighbours in need.
The Society of St. Vincent de Paul is dedicated to feeding, clothing, housing and healing individuals and families in our community who have nowhere else to turn for help. As important, SVdP provides meaningful opportunities for volunteers to serve their neighbours in need with love and compassion.
